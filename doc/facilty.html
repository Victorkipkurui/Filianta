Our Facilities

Overview of Our World-Class Facilities

At Filianta Hospital, we offer a wide range of advanced medical facilities designed to provide exceptional care to all our patients. Our facilities are equipped with cutting-edge technology and are staffed by experienced professionals to ensure the highest standards of care.

1. Emergency Department

Our 24/7 Emergency Department is fully equipped to handle any medical emergency with speed and precision. Our team of trained professionals is available around the clock to provide life-saving care, with advanced trauma care, emergency surgeries, and rapid diagnostics.

2. Surgical Suite

Our state-of-the-art surgical suite includes modern operating theaters with advanced technology to support a wide range of surgical procedures. From minimally invasive surgeries to complex operations, we ensure the highest standards of safety and precision in every procedure.

3. Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our Intensive Care Unit is designed to provide critical care for patients in need of constant monitoring and support. Equipped with the latest technology, our ICU offers specialized care for patients recovering from surgery, serious illnesses, or trauma.

4. Maternity and Neonatal Care

We provide comprehensive maternity and neonatal care services, ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for mothers and newborns. From prenatal care to postnatal support, our team of specialists is committed to providing the best care for mothers and babies alike.

5. Diagnostic Imaging and Radiology

Our Diagnostic Imaging and Radiology Department offers advanced imaging services such as MRI, CT scans, X-rays, and ultrasound. These services help in the accurate diagnosis of medical conditions, ensuring timely and appropriate treatment.

6. Outpatient Clinics

Filianta Hospital provides a wide range of outpatient services for consultations, diagnosis, and treatment. Our outpatient clinics offer specialized care across various fields, including cardiology, oncology, pediatrics, and more, all under one roof.

7. Rehabilitation and Physical Therapy

Our Rehabilitation and Physical Therapy Unit is designed to help patients recover from surgeries, injuries, and illnesses. Our experienced physical therapists work closely with patients to create personalized rehabilitation plans to help them regain strength and mobility.
