About Our Delivery Support

At Filianta Hospital, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care to expecting mothers and their newborns. Our team of experienced obstetricians, midwives, and nurses is here to ensure that your delivery experience is safe, comfortable, and personalized to your needs.

Our Services Include

  • Pre-delivery Care: Comprehensive prenatal care with regular checkups, ultrasounds, and consultations to monitor the health and progress of both mother and baby.
  • Labor and Delivery: State-of-the-art labor rooms equipped with modern facilities to ensure a smooth and safe delivery. Our team is skilled in both natural and assisted deliveries, with C-section capabilities available if needed.
  • Pain Management: We offer various pain relief options during labor, including epidurals, to ensure you have a comfortable delivery experience.
  • Postnatal Care: Post-delivery care for both mother and baby, including breastfeeding support, newborn checkups, and guidance on postnatal recovery.
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU): Our NICU is equipped to provide specialized care for newborns requiring additional medical attention after birth.

Why Choose Filianta Hospital for Delivery Support?

  • Experienced Team: Our obstetricians and midwives are highly experienced in handling all types of deliveries, from natural births to complex C-sections.
  • Personalized Care: We believe in individualized care for each mother, ensuring that your birth plan is respected and followed.
  • 24/7 Availability: Our delivery team is available around the clock to assist you whenever the time comes for your delivery.
  • Advanced Facilities: From private labor suites to our fully equipped NICU, we ensure the highest standards of care.
  • Family-Centered Approach: We encourage family involvement in the birth process and support bonding between mother, baby, and family members.

Preparing for Your Delivery

As your delivery date approaches, we recommend the following steps to ensure you are fully prepared:

  • Schedule a Hospital Tour: Familiarize yourself with our labor and delivery facilities. Contact us to schedule a tour of our maternity ward.
  • Create a Birth Plan: Discuss your preferences with your doctor, including pain relief options, delivery methods, and postnatal care.
