Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Filianta Hospital, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients' personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you in the following ways:

  • When you register for an appointment, consultation, or treatment.
  • When you visit our website or use our online services.
  • During interactions with healthcare professionals and staff.

The types of information we collect may include:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Medical history, treatment records, and other health-related information.
  • Insurance and billing information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Filianta Hospital uses your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide healthcare services and manage your treatment.
  • To process payments and manage billing.
  • To communicate with you regarding your appointments and medical care.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We will not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent or at your request.
  • With healthcare providers involved in your care.
  • For insurance and billing purposes.
  • To comply with legal requirements such as court orders or subpoenas.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include:

  • Using encryption and secure protocols for data transmission.
  • Implementing access controls to limit who can view your information.
  • Regularly updating our security practices to address emerging threats.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

  • The right to access your medical records and request corrections if necessary.
  • The right to request that your personal data be deleted, subject to legal obligations.
  • The right to limit or object to the use of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Cookies and Website Data

Filianta Hospital uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use certain features on our site.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will post the updated policy on our website and indicate the effective date at the top.
