Specialized Services at Filianta Hospital

Filianata Hospital is proud to offer a wide range of specialized medical services, ensuring the highest level of care for our patients. Our state-of-the-art facilities and highly trained staff make us a leading healthcare provider in the region.

Here are some of the specialized services we offer:

  • Cardiology: Our cardiology department offers comprehensive heart care, from diagnostic testing to advanced treatments for heart diseases.
  • Orthopedics: Specializing in joint replacement, sports injuries, and complex fractures, our orthopedic team provides expert care.
  • Neurology: We provide advanced diagnosis and treatment for neurological conditions, including stroke, epilepsy, and more.
  • Pediatrics: Our pediatric unit is dedicated to the health and well-being of children, offering specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents.
  • Oncology: Our oncology center provides cutting-edge treatments for cancer, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgery.
  • Maternity Services: We offer comprehensive maternity care, including prenatal, labor, delivery, and postpartum support in a family-friendly environment.

At Filianata Hospital, we are committed to delivering compassionate care with the latest medical advancements to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.
